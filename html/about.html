About Us

ETC, founded in 2024, is effect of collaboration of two students from the Silesian University of Technology: Dominik Stasiak and Łukasz Holeczek. Their shared passion for computer science, nurtured since childhood, has blossomed into a cutting-edge service provider for engineering firms.

What sets us apart is our insatiable appetite for knowledge. We're not content with the status quo; instead, we're constantly expanding our skillset, exploring new technologies, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in software development. This commitment to continuous learning ensures that our clients always benefit from the latest advancements in the field.

Our core values are the bedrock of our success:

  • Continuous Improvement: We embrace every challenge as an opportunity to grow and refine our skills, constantly raising the bar for ourselves and our industry.
  • Honesty and Transparency: We believe in building trust through open communication, ensuring our clients are always in the loop and confident in our processes.
  • Embracing Challenges: We thrive on complexity, viewing each difficult problem as a chance to showcase our creativity and technical prowess.
  • Client-Centric Approach: We measure our success by the success of our clients, tailoring our solutions to not just meet, but anticipate their needs.

At ETC, we're not just developing software; we're engineering the future, one line of code at a time. Join us on this exciting journey of innovation and discovery.
